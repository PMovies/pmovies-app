Founder & Editor-in-Chief, PMovies

Ahmed Khaled is a film critic and cinephile based in the UAE. He founded PMovies to create a space where film lovers can discover, discuss, and document their relationship with cinema — free from algorithm, free from noise.

His editorial focus centres on world cinema, independent film, and the international festival circuit: Cannes, Venice, TIFF, and beyond.
