PMovies — Independent Arabic Cinema Media

The Outlet

THE OUTLET

PMovies is an independent Arabic-language cinema publication and community platform. It began in December 2022 as a Twitter-based film community and launched as a fully built web platform at p-movies.com in 2026. It covers Egyptian and Arab film alongside international cinema — through editorial reviews, long-form filmmaker interviews, podcast programming, and community features including curated lists, fan art, and weekly digests.

The Editor

AK

Ahmed Khaled

Founder & Editor-in-Chief, PMovies

Egyptian, based in Doha, Qatar. He has written about cinema since 2022, covering nearly every film he watches across Letterboxd, Twitter/X, and p-movies.com. He hosts long-form filmmaker conversations, produces the monthly series هنتفرج على إيه, and built the PMovies platform independently.

He writes from feeling first — and believes honesty about cinema is more valuable than authority over it.
